How it works

1
You can view the range of goods on the Items
2
On the Items page you can select the product you are interested in
3
If the product is available for rent, then you can select the date you need
4
On the Checkout page, you need to select the project to which the products you purchased relate.
5
After confirming the delivery date and registration, a manager will contact you.
6
You can view all your orders, and their status on the Orders page, when the time for renting the goods is near the end, you can fill out a return form, after which the manager will contact you.
